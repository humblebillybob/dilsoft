The philosophy

"Good software is designed for the lifecycle, not just the launch."

Most companies treat infrastructure as a one-time project: build it, hand it off, move on. That model fails when things change, teams turn over, or the business grows faster than the system was designed to handle.

It is why Agent Analytics, a system Dilshad built in 2018, has run in production for over eight years with no engineering involvement. And it is why clients come back.
